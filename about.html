Our Story

Welcome to Pots & more, your go-to destination for homes for your fabulous plants! Nestled in the heart of the Edinburgh, our passionate local business is on a mission to bring you the finest, top-quality pots with a touch of quirky charm.

At Pots & more, we’re not just plant enthusiasts; we’re firm believers in the magical powers of greenery. We wholeheartedly embrace the mood-lifting and health-enhancing benefits that plants effortlessly bring to your humble abode or bustling workspace. We take immense pride in curating a delightful collection of simple yet contemporary pots and planters that flawlessly showcase your beloved plants.

With new stock arriving weekly, we’re always on the lookout for the next extraordinary addition to our ever-evolving collection. We’re constantly refining our range based on your feedback and the latest trends. Your satisfaction and happiness are our top priorities, and we’ll go above and beyond to ensure you find the perfect companion for your green space.

Don’t hesitate to reach out to our dedicated team of plant enthusiasts—we’re here to lend a helping hand. We proudly call ourselves “Plant Hunters,” so if you have a specific pot style in mind, consider us your trusty detectives.

Join us on this exciting green adventure at Plants & more, where passion meets professionalism, and quality reigns supreme. Together, let’s transform your space into a thriving oasis of natural beauty.
